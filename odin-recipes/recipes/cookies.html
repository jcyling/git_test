Cookies

This recipe is from the New York Times and passed to me by my good friend N.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups minus 2 tablespoons cake flour (8 1/2 ounces)
  • 1 ⅔ cups bread flour (8 1/2 ounces)
  • 1 ¼ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons coarse salt
  • 1 ¼ cups unsalted butter (2 1/2 sticks)
  • 1 ¼ cups light brown sugar (10 ounces)
  • 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons granulated sugar (8 ounces)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons natural vanilla extract
  • 1 ¼ pounds bittersweet chocolate disks or fèves, at least 60 percent cacao content
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. Mix wet ingredients
  2. Mix dry ingredients
  3. Combine! Then roll the dough into small cookie balls.
  4. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees and put cookie dough balls in on baking paper.
  5. Take them out after 10 minutes.
